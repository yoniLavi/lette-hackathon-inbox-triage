## The Challenge

### Sponsor Platform - Lette AI

Property managers deal with large volumes of communication from tenants, landlords, contractors, and prospects, often in messy, unstructured formats.

Your challenge is to build a system that helps process that communication intelligently by:

- identifying who messages are from
- understanding context across threads
- assessing urgency
- surfacing clear recommended actions

The goal is to help a property manager quickly understand what matters most and what they should do next.

JSON for the challenge is at proptech-test-data.json
